How it works

From a listing that didn't sell to a responsibly recycled item — in four simple steps.

Step 1

Try to swap first

Post your item on Swappo. Most things find a new owner in days.

Step 2

Can't swap? No problem

If your item is too worn or specialized, head to our Recycle Hub.

Step 3

Find the right partner

We match your item type with the best recycling center in your emirate.

Step 4

Drop off or schedule pickup

Visit the center or book a free pickup with our partners. Done.
