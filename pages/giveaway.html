📋 Gift Corner Rules

  • Claims limited by your plan — Free: 1/month, Swappo Pro: 5/month

  • Give-to-Unlock: complete 1 swap OR publish 1 gift before claiming

  • Cash or card payments are never involved — Gift Corner items are 100% free
