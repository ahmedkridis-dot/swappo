13.1 Dynamic QR Code + Geolocation Confirmation

To confirm a physical swap, each user generates a dynamic QR code from the app at the meetup. The QR code is valid for a short time window and can only be scanned if both devices are physically within a radius of approximately 200 metres of each other, verified by device geolocation. This double check (QR + geolocation) is used to:

  • Confirm the swap was genuinely completed in person
  • Unlock the rating and badge progression system (see Sections 14 and 16)
  • Prevent fake completion reports that would artificially inflate reputation

By scanning the other party's QR code, you confirm that you have received the agreed item(s) and any cash top-up in person. The scan is a non-refutable confirmation of physical exchange.

13.2 Delivery-Based Confirmation (Phase 2)

If the swap is shipped via an upcoming Swappo Delivery partner (Phase 2 feature, not yet active), confirmation will be performed automatically once both shipments are marked as delivered by the courier's tracking system. Users will have a short window to report any issue after delivery.
